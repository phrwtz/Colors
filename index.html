Instructions

The object of this game is to turn as many of the hexagons as possible white. You do this by dragging the colored hexagonal tiles to target tiles of a different color. When this happens the target tile changes color and the moving tile leaves behind a white hexagon. The legal moves are a follows: a blue tile placed on a red target tile turns it purple, a red tile placed oh a yellow target tile turns it orange, and a yellow tile placed on a blue target tile turns it green. When all three primary colors are present the tile turns white; thus a red tile placed on a green tile, a blue tile on an orange tile, and a yellow tile on a purple tile all become white.

Adjacent tiles of the same color act as "roads" for moving tiles, white tiles or tiles that are not legal target tiles act as barricades. A tile can move in any direction along tiles of the same color before reaching a target tile that it can mix with. If that tile is also part of a contiguous group of tiles of the same color the moving tile can continue to move in any direction along those adjacent tiles before being placed on top of one of them (and changing its color). However, once a tile has thus selected a target color it cannot "change its mind" and move to another color even if that color is a legal target.
